This is James Hernandez

UX Designer and Creative based in Austin, Texas

I am an educator turned UX designer who's driven by curiosity and passionate about design. I love font and color and will spend hours, maybe days, deciding which one to use. OK, maybe only a little bit of that last part is true.

My unique skill-set lets me bring a teaching perspective and creative problem-solving approach to create user interactions and experiences people love to use. Persistence, curiosity and an obsessive attention to detail define me. With a background in teaching, and experience as a digital designer and developer, I excel in communication and competent in all aspects of the design process.
